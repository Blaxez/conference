View on Google Maps

How to Reach

By Air

Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport (LKO) is well connected to major cities. The venue is approximately 30 km from the airport.

By Train

Lucknow Charbagh Railway Station (LKO) is the main station, about 15 km from the university.

By Road

Lucknow is well connected by road. The university is located on Sitapur Road, easily accessible by taxi or auto-rickshaw.

Nearby Attractions

  • Bara Imambara
  • Rumi Darwaza
  • Ambedkar Park
  • Hazratganj Market
