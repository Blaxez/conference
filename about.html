About ICRAME 2026

Fostering innovation and collaboration at the intersection of multidisciplinary engineering.

About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advancements and Innovation in Engineering & Technology (ICRAME 2026) aims to create a global platform that brings together leading researchers, academicians, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss emerging innovations across multidisciplinary engineering domains. With rapid advancements in artificial intelligence, automation, sustainable technologies, and digital transformation, the conference provides opportunities to explore new research directions and address real-world engineering challenges.

The purpose of this conference is to facilitate knowledge sharing and foster research collaborations by uniting experts from computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, and agricultural technologies under one umbrella. The conference emphasizes the convergence of traditional engineering with cutting-edge innovations such as AI, smart devices, IoT, renewable energy systems, autonomous systems, and computational modelling.

A key feature of ICRAME 2026 is its strong multidisciplinary orientation. Unlike traditional conferences that remain confined to singular domains, this event promotes cross-disciplinary solutions to modern engineering problems. The conference highlights applied research through case studies, prototypes, and practical demonstrations, ensuring that the proceedings offer readers both theoretical depth and real-world relevance.

The conference also prioritizes global participation. More than 25% of submissions and attendees are expected from international universities, including Europe, the United States, Russia, and Southeast Asia. Keynote speakers from abroad will contribute chapters to the proceedings, ensuring an international academic standard. This global outreach makes the conference distinct from many national-level events in similar fields.

The intended audience includes researchers, faculty members, postgraduate students, policymakers, R&D professionals, and engineers seeking updated knowledge on state-of-the-art technologies. The proceedings will serve as an essential reference for emerging engineering areas and can be incorporated into advanced engineering curricula at the postgraduate level.

Another special aspect of the conference is its emphasis on sustainable development and engineering for societal impact. Tracks such as renewable energy, environmental engineering, smart agriculture, and intelligent healthcare technologies reinforce the role of engineering in addressing global challenges.

Finally, ICRAME 2026 is committed to maintaining high academic and editorial standards. All submitted papers undergo a rigorous double-blind peer-review process, plagiarism screening, and technical checks as per international publishing guidelines. The collaboration with Taylor & Francis ensures global dissemination and indexing, maximizing visibility and long-term academic value.

Book Synopsis

“Recent Advances in Multidisciplinary Engineering: Integrating Science, Technology, and Innovation” presents a comprehensive collection of high-quality research contributions discussed during the International Conference on Recent Advancements and Innovation in Engineering & Technology (ICRAME 2026). The volume showcases original research articles spanning emerging areas including artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things, machine learning, smart manufacturing, civil infrastructure innovations, biomedical technologies, precision agriculture, renewable energy systems, and cybersecurity.

The book offers an interdisciplinary perspective, bridging theoretical advancements with practical applications and industry solutions. Contributions come from national and international universities, R&D institutions, and industry leaders, ensuring diverse and global insights. Each chapter is carefully peer-reviewed and selected based on academic rigor, novelty, and relevance to contemporary engineering challenges.

This volume is designed for researchers, faculty, and practitioners in engineering and applied sciences, as well as postgraduate students and policymakers. It also serves as a valuable resource for professionals working in digital transformation, automation, energy, materials science, agriculture, healthcare technology, and computing domains.

The proceedings highlight cross-disciplinary research trends, real-world case studies, and future directions in engineering innovation, making it a significant reference for academic libraries, technical institutions, and global engineering communities.

We hereby confirm that all papers submitted to the ICRAME 2026 conference proceedings are original, unpublished, and not under consideration for publication elsewhere. Authors are required to submit a signed copyright and originality declaration at the time of submission. Any manuscript found to be previously published or concurrently submitted to another venue will be removed from the review process and rejected. The proceedings will include only those papers that pass the double-blind peer review and meet the originality and ethical standards mandated by the conference and the publisher.
