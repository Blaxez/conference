About the Conference

ICRAME 2026 is a global forum bringing together researchers, academicians, innovators, and industry experts to discuss cutting-edge developments across engineering disciplines.

The conference promotes cross-domain collaboration, emphasizing the convergence of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture.

  • Proceedings published with Taylor & Francis (Routledge)
  • Double-blind peer review with mandatory Turnitin check (<15%)
  • Post-conference webinars and global visibility programs

Conference Tracks

Covering five major pillars of multidisciplinary engineering innovation.

Computer Science

AI, ML, Cloud Computing, IoT, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Big Data, Software Engineering, Computer Vision.

Electrical & Electronics

Smart Grids, Embedded Systems, VLSI, 5G/6G, Power Electronics, Signal Processing.

Mechanical Eng.

Robotics, CAD/CAM, Smart Materials, Mechatronics, Manufacturing Technologies.

Civil Engineering

Smart Cities, Sustainable Structures, GIS, Environmental Engineering, Infrastructure Systems.

AI in Agriculture

Precision Farming, Drones, Agri-Robotics, Soil & Crop Analytics, Climate Modelling.
